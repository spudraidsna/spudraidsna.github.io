Welcome

Welcome the the Spud Raids NA reference site!


Here you'll be able to find quick build overviews and links to their respective Snowcrows build guides.


Please keep in mind that the information on these build overviews is purely our opinion and aligns with what we usually run on our training runs. We do not create exhaustive build guides so always refer to Snowcrows or your preferred resource for meta build info.


Also note that this site as a whole is a personal project for us. None of us are professional developers; we are hobbyists at most. So please excuse our design/code mistakes. And please feel free to let us know if you come across anything that you feel should be fixed - we'll try our best to work it out.


We recommend viewing this site on either the latest Chrome or the latest Firefox. The site is unfortunately not mobile friendly right now so if things look funky on your phone/tablet, that's probably why.
